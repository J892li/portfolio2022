Background

People with depression or anxiety are often unable to relive themselves when stressed or frustrated. The goal is to reduce stress and anxiety for the high mental stress population using the method of mindfulness training in an immersive environment. Yes, meditation under the sea. 

As the PM for this project, we completed this project within 3 months using the waterfall process. 

Goal：
How to reduce stress and improve well-being and performance among city dwellers  

Target audiences :
People with high stress and anxiety

How to play the game ？

We designed the experience so that the player can stay under the sea and play with sea animals. The narrator/voice-over will guide the player through the journey, to practice mediation.

Challenges 1 - Design for comfort

Originally, we considered designing the game with a fabulous UI style. However, considering the limitation to focus on fancy graphics, players cannot focus on the most important information, and the UI tends to be out of sight for the players to interact with.

Solution:
I aligned the issue with the team and we switched to diabetic UI with low transparency. Then,we focused on design for the standard line of sight. Yet we kept all the UI within the most comfort zone with best sight sitting. We designed the experience so that the player can go through the journey in the most comfort way, to practice mediation.

Challenges 2 - Design for Usability 

In the original design, we used voice as the cue to tell the player what to do during the game. However, one problem with Oculus is that the toggle button is hard to find for new users, even with voice cues.

Solution: We thought about using UI for indication in the first place. However, this seems to be confusing as well. Eventually, we re-designed the audio narration to guide the user through the journey.
