Background 


Topic: For the long time (RSA brief 7, 2020)

Design is the interface between technology and people, yet there is little study of either.
How might we encourage people and communities to think and act for the long term?
How can we reshape rituals and ceremonies to empower our next generation?


Issue: China will run out of land resources for cemetery space within the next ten years. Therefore the government has encouraged biodegradable burial practice. However, people's burial traditions have not changed accordingly.

Solution: We designed a concept product : Soul Garden, a mobile platform using AI and machine learning technologies to change people perception towards death and change the tradition of our community in the future.

Challenges

#1 Technology Viability
We want to focus on changing the relationship between the living and the deceased to mitigate the influence of traditional values. We designed a product which can generate an AI replica to interact with users both visually and verbally. 
Solution: AI + Machine learning technologies
While users uploaded files, machine learning algorithm with extract information and generated an AI replica.


#2 Plan the design direction
Solution : Rapid prototyping 


#3 Prototype testing
Solution : Remote testing

We conducted a remote user testing which involved 8 users. 

The feedback is simple: Bigger buttons, shorter tasks flow and Do not make me think!

Based on the feedback, I revised three versions of the interface design. As a result, we published our product. It is an App with 40+ interfaces and many hard works from my peers. You can try the prototype here

Summary

Reflection

Successes:
We revised three times based on user feedback; it was beneficial.

Things I Would Do Differently next time:

I believe I will try more on conducting user research next time since the majority of my work is about UI design and product viability.
